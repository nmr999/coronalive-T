Overview

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.
In early 2020, after a December 2019 outbreak in China, the World Health Organization identified SARS-CoV-2 as a new type of coronavirus.
The COVID-19 virus spreads primarily through droplets of saliva or discharge from the nose when an infected person coughs or sneezes, so it’s important that you also practice respiratory etiquette.


Symptoms

Most common symptoms :- fever. dry cough. tiredness.
Less common symptoms :- aches and pains. sore throat. diarrhoea. conjunctivitis. headache. loss of taste or smell.
